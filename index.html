ZILCH

Zilch, or Zilchdeals Inc., is a company I started during my junior year summer. It's an E-coupons company that offers discounts of up to 99% on grocery items approaching its sell-by date. All prices are up to the store's discretion, and the food is as safe to eat as when regularly sold. I sold my company to Siva Systems this December (2015) for $100,000.

OLEATI

Oleati is a business plan I have been working on since my freshman year. It is an oil company that sells heating oil produced from depolymerizing non-recyclable waste plastics. It has won international recognition (3rd) at DECA's International Career Development Conference. I am currently in the process of starting the company.

DECA

DECA is an internationally recognized competitive business club aiming to create opportunities that enhance business knowledge and foster the entrepreneurial spirit amongst high school students. As Vice President of my school's DECA Chapter, I oversee all competitive aspects of the club, and lead the Competitive Team (four Directors, six Captains). I also helped code the Lynbrook DECA Website.

BITKAR

Bitkar Inc. is a startup that acts as a one-stop shop for booking auto repairs, buying new auto parts, and finding auto parts dealers depending on the make and model of your car. When I interned for Bitkar during my junior year summer, I mainly worked on adding new features to their website and app, and consolidated their databases.

ELECROLYZER

During my junior year winter break, while interning at the Jawaharlal Nehru Technological University, I conducted a research experiment regarding the efficiency of producing Hydrogen gas, and I analyzed the effect of adding a Carbon Nanotube catalyst to a PEM (Proton-Exchange Membrane) Electrolyzer.

GBM

During my freshman year summer, I interned at the UC San Francisco Carcinogenics Department under the supervision of Dr. Joanna J. Phillips as a Cancer Research Intern. There, I conducted a research study on the effect of particular genes on the Glioblastoma Multiform (GBM) condition.

TECH CHALLENGE

During my freshman year, I was a part of a Tech Challenge team, Cometz 141. Our task was to build a device that could launch eggs onto three areas of different terrains, distances, elevations, and angles of slope. We also had to build a capsule that could safeguard the egg from cracks and leaks upon impact. Our team won 1st place overall, and we were invited to the Tech Awards Gala.
